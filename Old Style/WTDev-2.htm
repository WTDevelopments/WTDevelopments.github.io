Arduino Software and Android Remote Control for large LED screen.

Hello everyone! I share with you my most recent project, which was given as a donation and contribution to the School of Technical Education No. 2 (Escuela Técnica 2 Junín). This is software designed to control a giant LED screen, with the ability to add amazing effects and animations. In addition, the necessary programming has been provided so that students can carry out their own projects in a simple way, creating their own animations intuitively.

You can know all my projects in this web, from the upper menu. I'am appreciate that you take some time to see them.
